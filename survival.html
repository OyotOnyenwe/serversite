Onyenwe Survival

Onyenwe Survival is a peaceful Vanilla+ survival Minecraft experience aimed at simplicty and enjoyment. We employ plugins that offer a simplified and improved Minecraft experience.

Quality of Life

Survival uses the Harbor sleep plugin to allow players to skip the night when a majority of the players are sleeping instead of all players being required to sleep. It features chat messages and a boss bar to explain the functionality in an aesthetically pleasing manner.

The EssentialsX plugin is used to allow for simple but elegant teleportation commands such as /home, /back, and /tpa.

Most importantly, the GriefPrevention plugin is used to allow players to claim land and ensure the safety of their items and builds.

Rules

  1. NO cheats or hacks are allowed.
  2. Claim abuse is not allowed.
  3. Keep toxicity to a minimum.
  4. Do not deliberately try to lag or crash the server.
  5. Do not try to circumvent administrative systems such as chat filters or anticheat.
  6. Interpretation of the rules is up to the staff.
