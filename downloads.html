World Downloads

When any of the servers has a world reset, the previous world will be available for download on this page.

The world downloads are hosted on Google Drive. Files are compressed in .7z archive format and can be opened with 7-zip. Once extracted, place the folder in your %appdata%/.minecraft/saves directory.

Downloads are very large, so do not close this tab or the browser until they are finished.
